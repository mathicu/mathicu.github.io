感谢@黄静思等人提供的勘误信息！

   $$
   f\left(x_{1}\right)+f\left(x_{2}\right)=2 f\left(x_{0}\right)+\frac{\left(x_{1}-x_{2}\right)^{2}}{\color{red}8}\left(f^{\prime \prime}\left(\xi_{1}\right)+f^{\prime \prime}\left(\xi_{2}\right)\right),
